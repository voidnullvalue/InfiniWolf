# InfiniWolf Roadmap

## Secret discovery and fancy secrets

- Target 15% of ordinary secrets behind visually normal wall tiles; keep the
  remaining 85% behind readable decorated-wall hints.
- Compensate for the weaker visual hint by increasing the expected pickup
  value of normal-wall secrets. Required progression items and mandatory
  solvency supplies must never depend on finding one.
- Add a rare **fancy secret** form, capped at one per episode. Fancy secrets
  exist only behind normal walls and remain entirely optional.
- Broaden fancy-secret geometry beyond the standard pocket: possibilities
  include a carefully decorated small room or a bounded optional level wing
  with guards, doors, and a substantial cache of extra pickups.
- Weight the second chamber of a nested secret toward becoming the episode's
  fancy secret. Its first chamber must remain safe and optional, and opening
  either depth must never bypass locks or become necessary to finish the map.
- Record entrance-hint class, reward tier, nesting depth, and fancy-secret
  ownership in the manifest. Add campaign validation for the 15% aggregate
  target, the one-per-episode cap, normal-wall-only fancy entrances, complete
  reachability, lock isolation, and nonessential progression status.
